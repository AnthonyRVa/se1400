What Do Others Have That I Should?

What others have a lot of on their front page is images. The very first time you enter their websites your greeted with images of their work.This is something I want to have done with my own page. Another feature other websites have are links to a more in depth disccusion of their work. The links are stored in images you can click on which is somethings I'd like to do for my website.

What Doesn't Work On Other Websites?

To me, what doesn't work on other websites is when the top bar follows the user as they scroll down. In my case I find it annoying as it tend to get in the way of image and makes the whole screen feel tight.
