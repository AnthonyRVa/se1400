Website Summary:

What content will the site contain?

The website will contain a lot of images in order to showcase my work and skill. There will be text about talking about myself, but primarily showing off my work.

Where will you get all of the content for your site (ie. images & text)?

Most of the content will be created by me. I will be screen shotting and recording my work, with only a few of images of myself from my phone camera.

Why should users care about the focus of the site?

The reason why users should care about the focus of my site is because it'll inform them about everything they need to know about me. The things I can, and have, done. My past, present, and future work

Why should a site be built for this?

The need for this site stems from my career. In my career as a game developer I have to be able to show off my work. That can be done with this website hence the need for it.

What would you like users to do at your site?

What people will do at my site is primarily look at my work. Besides that, they might read a little about me, and check my socials.

What is the goal of the site?

The goal of this sight is to show of my work and skills to others. It's meant to appeal primarily to employers and grab their attention. A second use purpose for this site is to document my work. Doing so would allow me to look back and see what I have done or how I've improved.
