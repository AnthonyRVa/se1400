Monster Bounties


Monster Bounties Is a game still in development. The premise is you go to seven different map's to hunt, or capture, seven unique cryptids. This games fit's the description of a survival co-op horror game. I've been with this project for a year and counting, with a small group of 6 people. I have learnt, and continue to, learn many skills from this project which also serves as place where I can practice said skills.

This project has taught me a lot of skills outside of engine as a result of working in a team. I've had the chance to manage a team of 3 people and during that time I learned the value of time management and how to keep everyone on pace. Experience like that, and others, have taught how to effectively communicate with my colleagues and how to delegate tasks, check up on the teams progress, and plan for a group.

Death's Door

Death's Door is a side project I'm doing with 2 other people. In this game I am the lead programmer, creative director, and animator. In the game you play as an elderly person bound to a wheel chair In a assisted living facility.

You awake one day to find that everyone is gone. You soon see a room that leads to a place you've long forgotten. While on this journey through your memories you discover your being hunter, and with no clear goal you go through all the doorways into your memory, until the end.
